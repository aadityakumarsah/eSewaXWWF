# ScamShield — Hackathon Pitch

## The Problem
- Nepal lost ₹2.3B to digital scams in 2025
- 73% of eSewa users receive phishing attempts monthly
- Elderly and first-time users are hit hardest
- Current solution: reactive (report after loss)

## Our Solution
ScamShield is a gamified scam awareness platform that teaches users to recognize scams BEFORE they fall victim. Think Duolingo for digital safety.

## How It Works
1. Users face 10 realistic scenarios (SMS, calls, QR codes, fake apps)
2. They decide: Legit or Scam?
3. Instant feedback teaches red flags
4. Badges, streaks, leaderboards drive engagement

## Why This Wins
- **For eSewa**: Integrate as onboarding step → fewer scam support tickets → higher trust
- **For WWF**: Extend model to wildlife poaching awareness
- **For users**: Protect themselves and their families
- **For Nepal**: National-scale digital literacy tool

## Business Model
- Free for users (core)
- eSewa white-labels into app as "Security Academy"
- B2B: Banks, NGOs, government partnerships
- Premium tier: Advanced scenarios, family accounts

## Traction Path
- Week 1: Launch at hackathon
- Month 1: 10k users organic
- Month 3: eSewa integration pilot
- Year 1: 1M+ Nepali users protected
